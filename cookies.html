Cookie Notice

This Cookie Notice explains how Auxin Ventures uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand user behavior, and enhance overall experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the site to function properly and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us analyze site traffic and improve performance, typically through anonymous data collection (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: These may remember your preferences (like language settings) to personalize your experience.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Understand how visitors interact with the site
  • Improve functionality and performance
  • Remember cookie preferences (e.g., acceptance of our banner)

4. Managing Cookies

You can choose to accept or reject cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies entirely. Please note that disabling cookies may affect site performance or availability of features.

5. Changes to This Notice

We may update this notice as our use of cookies evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about our cookie use, please contact us at info@auxinventures.com.
